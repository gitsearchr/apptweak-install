What is AppTweak?

AppTweak is a modern package manager, built using Swift, designed to be fast, reliable and sleek. You can use it in conjuction with APT repos to install packages, free or paid, to your jailbroken iOS device.

What jailbreaks come with AppTweak?

Taurine, Odyssey, Odysseyra1n and Chimera come with Sileo preinstalled!



Is AppTweak open source?

AppTweak is fully open source. Details on how you can contribute to the Sileo project can be found in the readme!



Can I use AppTweak with checkra1n or unc0ver?

Yes, you can! Add repo.getsileo.app to your sources!
